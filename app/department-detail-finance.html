Improve control of your financial operations, including expense claims, budgeting, billing and more, with our financial services software. K2 business applications can help you locate gaps in your financial framework, protect your current revenue streams, and identify new business opportunities.

Solutions

Within the financial operations department, organizations face more pressure than ever to identify gaps in their financial framework to better manage budgets and costs. K2's financial services software can help ensure you maintain better control of your financial operations by incorporating line-of-business (LOB) data into customized applications that can be built without code. Making great decisions in line with proper governance, compliance and risk mitigation requires financial workflows, streamlined processes and increased visibility.

Budgeting

Improve budgeting and forecasting with applications that enable you to bring data from your disparate LOB systems into a single view, providing budget owners with the information they need to make well-informed estimates and recommendations.

Expense claim management

Ensure your expense claims are in compliance with company policies and authorized by the right people with automated workflows that enable you to approve and track expense claims and reports that help you evaluate trends for faster expense processing and improved projections.

Loan origination system

Build and customize a loan origination system that conforms to your specific requirements and procedures. Loan origination steps can vary with different loan types, risks, regulators, lender policies, and other factors. K2's financial services software can incorporate all of these variables into a process that reduces bottlenecks.

Internal auditing

Relieve pressure placed on audit teams with applications and enterprise risk management software that gives you complete visibility into your finance and business processes, as well as full auditing capabilities that make it possible to archive data even after a workflow has been deleted.

Invoice processing software

Automate, simplify and speed up your invoicing process through automated financial workflows and approval processes that route invoices to the right people and systems, so that each invoice is correctly classified, validated and safely stored in a system of record.
