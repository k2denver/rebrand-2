Transform IT processes with K2

Set your IT department apart and improve client satisfaction with forms and workflows that streamline your IT processes, from support tickets to help desk management.

Solutions

Expectations on IT departments are increasing, with teams asked to help shape the business strategy and provide an integrated approach to operations, rather than just support technology requests. This means that IT departments need to make processes such as IT service management and support ticket systems as efficient as possible, so they have time to focus on more strategic endeavors. K2 business applications increase process visibility, eliminate redundancy and reduce manual errors, improving client satisfaction and giving you the time you need to focus on more important things.

IT service management

Reduce the workload on IT through self-help portals, service request management applications and reporting features that provide added visibility into IT processes and service requests.

Support tickets

Improve client satisfaction with an automated support ticket system with workflows and forms that facilitate ticket submission and tracking while easing the burden on IT.

Help desk

Streamline your help desk process and provide internal and external clients a way to easily submit help desk requests and maintain visibility into the status of their request until the issue has been resolved.

Computer setup requests

Facilitate collaboration between HR and IT for new computer requests and other IT support needs, and create workflows that integrate with email clients and other cloud services to get employees up and running faster.
