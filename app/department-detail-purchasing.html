Improve visibility and insight into your purchasing processes and e-procurement system workflow while streamlining negotiations and services.

Solutions

From procurement software to vendor management systems, K2 can help alleviate the challenges that purchasing departments face by streamlining your procurement workflow, speeding up approvals, making RFP automation a reality and giving you easier access to critical information that will help you make better purchasing decisions. K2's purchase order software makes it easier to monitor and manage e-procurement system processes through forms and workflows that help improve efficiency and cut costs by streamlining simple to complex purchasing processes.

E-Procurement system

Easily manage processes involved in the online sale of products, goods and services with an efficient e-procurement system. Applications and procurement workflows streamline the buyer’s experience and reduce manual errors and bottlenecks.

Spend management

Bring together real-time information on spend analysis, sourcing, procurement, receiving, payments and account management to track and manage spend across your organization.

Vendor contract management

Collaborate, track and manage vendor contracts by centralizing information in a vendor management system. Use workflows and RFP automation tools to control the vendor contract lifecycle and ensure contracts are signed and renewed on time.

Requisition processing

Provide buyers with the tools they need to make better purchasing decisions, follow policies, and streamline requisition processes for verification and approval.

"It used to take around a week for POs to be raised. If everyone is at his or her desk, the approval of a PO can now be achieved in as little as 10 minutes.ʺ
Filip Legény, IT Project Manager, Dalkia
Dalkia, an energy and environmental company that raises 10,000 to 12,000 invoices in Slovakia each year, found itself struggling with a manual purchase order management process. Not only did the process take too much time, but engineers could unknowingly raise POs for parts that had been discontinued or get a digit wrong and order the wrong part. Using purchase order software and procurement workflow from K2, Dalkia automated the process, which reduced manual errors, increased visibility into which items the company had in stock, and saved the company a great deal of time and money.
