Build and run
business applications
for sales and marketing

Fill the funnel and drive sales with K2. From campaign management to collateral development, K2 business process management apps can increase efficiency and create customized solutions for sales and marketing processes across your organization.

Solutions

Finding the right solutions to support your organization’s unique range of sales and marketing needs can be challenging. Since information across teams is often stored in multiple systems, sales management software that can truly support sales-driven organizations should be able to pull data from disparate systems into solutions that can be tied to forms and workflows. With K2, you can do all of this and more – without code – to create customized solutions for sales and marketing processes across your organization. K2's sales and marketing management software makes it possible to automate steps to ensure that the right people are notified when tasks are completed, approvals and authorizations are obtained easily, and managers have the visibility they need.

Sales order management system

Save time and money by using a K2 powered order management system to streamline your sales order process. With K2, you can generate sales orders and have full visibility into the status of each process, with automated alerts that ensure orders are completed in a timely manner.

Campaign and marketing management software

Collaborate on campaigns, internally and externally, with marketing forms and workflows that facilitate approvals and performance tracking of messaging, media, ad buys and more.

Proposal and collateral development

Use K2 to manage document versions, facilitate review cycles, and speed up approval processes, with forms and workflows that ensure each proposal is routed to the right person, at the right time.

Customer self-service

Empower your customers by providing them access to a self-service order management system. Customers can place orders and track them, and also have the ability to submit and track issues they have with an order or product for an improved sense of visibility and control. K2 gives you more than your traditional customer management software.

Transforming sales and marketing, your way

K2 applications – composed of forms, workflow and data – can smooth out your sales and marketing processes and make it easier to collaborate across people and systems to manage deliverables and deadlines. With deep integration into backend systems, like Microsoft SharePoint, SAP, Salesforce and more, K2 brings the data you need into a sales management software solution for better lead tracking and analysis, and routes it to right people to streamline and speed up the sales cycle.
Content management
Manage the flow of content between parties to ensure the right messages and content are driven through the right channels at the right time.
Customer research and tracking
Monitor and track information across internal and external systems with customer management software, and take action based upon predetermined triggers.
Event planning
Keep everyone informed and in sync with tasks, reminders, information and reporting to ensure your event is a success.
Quote to cash
Drive a consistent process that includes pricing, quote creation, LOB integration, contract management, invoicing and payment receipt.
Contract lifecycle management
Make it easy for customers to renew contracts without the need for additional sales involvement.
Social interaction management
Task, manage and track social engagement across corporately owned assets and owners.
